You may or may not have heard about the recent decision by a number of [members of the Star Trek community](https://www.facebook.com/TrekAgainstTrumpOfficial/ "Trek Against Trump on Facebook") to pen an open letter urging voters to reject Donald Trump. This is obviously nonsense. No true Trekkie would accept this. There are any number of reasons for fans of the show and its creators to support Donald Trump for president. Here are just a few.
