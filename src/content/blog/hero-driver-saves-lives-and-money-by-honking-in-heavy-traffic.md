---
title: "Hero driver saves lives and money by honking in heavy traffic"
pubDate: 2016-09-27
tags: ["Incredible human achievements", "courage", "genius", "heroism", "problem solving", "traffic"]
---

Despite the apparent hopelessness of the situation, an anonymous driver stuck in a Monday evening rush hour traffic jam showed both fortitude and know-how when he or she expertly resolved the gridlock situation by unleashing a flurry of horn honks. Despite having no line of site to the actual cause of the slow-down, this paragon of driving prowess was able to send crystal clear directions to those who needed them by endless pounding on the horn of the 2009 Toyota Corolla he or she was driving.

Not only did this incredible accomplishment save millions of dollars by swiftly resolving the traffic jam and allowing everyone to get to their destinations at least thirty seconds faster than they otherwise would have, it also saved perhaps dozens of lives.

The expert horn honking also guided several lost pets to their owners, and cured at least one heretofore untreatable disease.
