---
title: "Woman heroically doesn’t murder coworker"
pubDate: 2016-10-07
tags: ["Incredible human achievements", "courage", "heroism", "restraint", "sexism", "strength"]
---

A young female professional has made history this week after she heroically refrained from brutally murdering her male co-worker after he condescendingly asked her why she cares so much about how she looks. Where any average woman would have rightfully stabbed him repeatedly for acting like the gendered double standard of how you have to look to be valued and respected in society was something that she could single handedly correct, Claire Harmon showed superhuman restraint by simply rolling her eyes and going back to her work routine.

As if this was not enough, the man then told her that she was beautiful no matter what she wore, as if his opinion was wanted and valid. Instead of slowly shaving the skin from his body with her dull scissors, she politely told him that he was an asshole and that he should leave, like a living saint.

While she did smash his hand to a bloody pulp after he put it on in her knee and told her that negativity wasn’t cute, the fact that he survived the encounter without further damage is testament to her incredible ability to turn the other cheek.
