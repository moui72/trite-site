---
title: "Black-on-black crime invalidates BLM completely"
pubDate: 2016-09-25
tags: ["TRUTH", "fact check", "racism", "the twitters"]
---

The simple fact is that police shootings of unarmed black people, including  
children, are unfortunate but necessary side effects of police doing their job:  
serving and protecting white people, and preventing them from encountering  
things that make them uncomfortable, like unarmed black people, including  
children. And even if this were not the case, if here or there a murder is  
committed by the police, I don’t see how so-called “Black Lives Matter” (BLM)  
activists can get so upset. I mean, mistakes happen. Haven’t you ever  
accidentally taken the life of an innocent person because you didn’t have time  
to eat breakfast one morning?

I mean, a cop kills a black person and is severely punished with paid leave, and  
people take to the streets with their signs and to the twitters with their  
hashtags; but when a black person kills a white and is sentenced to  
[20% to 60% more jail time than a white person would be](https://web.archive.org/web/20180902073057/http://newsone.com/1859475/black-people-receive-60-longer-sentences-for-same-crimes/),  
you don’t see white people going out and protesting!

And furthermore, the complaints of BLM are totally nullified by the fact that  
sometimes black people kill other black people. I mean, do we not live in an  
equal rights society? If they can do it, why can’t cops? Killing black people is  
like saying the “n” word – black people go around saying it all the time, so  
it’s perfectly OK for white people to say it too.
