---
title: "Strongest man alive carries wheely cart down stairs"
pubDate: 2016-09-28
tags: ["Incredible human achievements", "heroism", "laundry", "strength", "wheely carts"]
---

Displaying yet another incredible feat of strength, the strongest man alive recently carried a large wheely cart, which was almost entirely full of dirty clothes and laundry supplies, from his third story apartment to the street.

![Almost full wheely cart.](https://web.archive.org/web/20180902073303im_/http://trite.site/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/IMG_20160926_151604-300x216.jpg)

Almost full wheely cart.

Where most would have faltered, he endured. Unfazed by the enormity of the task, he simply took one step at a time, and achieved the impossible.

“I do this every week,” he said, sheepishly. “My friends tell me I should take two trips, but I’m like: I’ve gotta live up to my reputation as the strongest living human. The strongest living human doesn’t take two trips.”

When asked if he would also carry it back up, he said he wasn’t sure. “I’ll have to see how I am feeling. Sometimes I can carry it up all at once, but sometimes I have to take two trips.”

Several athletic apparel endorsement deals are apparently in the works.
