This may be an over discussed topic, but I felt I simply had to add my thoughts on the matter to the discourse at large. So here are ***my*** top 7 reasons why everyone should hate babies.

# 1. They are lazy

Babies are seriously the laziest people that there are. Have you ever seen a baby helping clean up after a dinner party? Nope, they are always the ones sleeping in their chair while everyone else is cleaning up. Have you ever seen a baby eat on its own? Most of them wrong even chew their own food! Worst of all, if you put a baby into bed (yeah, they won’t even get in bed under their own power) face down, they would rather suffocate than roll over on their own. Talk about lazy.

# 2. They are selfish

Besides being lazy, babies are also selfish. If even the slightest thing doesn’t go their way they’ll start crying about it until someone fixes it for them. And will they tell you what is wrong? No! It’s up to you to try and guess what they want just to shut them up. It didn’t matter if you had a hard day or your such or your trying to eat lunch or whatever. It’s always all about them.

# 3. They are hideous

Babies typically go with one of two hairstyles: pubic or fungal. Both are disgusting. And I don’t usually go in for body shaming, but talk about undulating little bags of flab. Plus, their arms and legs look like gross little bulbous tentacles. Ew, babies. Get your shit together.

# 4. They are heartless

If you are coming to death, a baby wouldn’t move a muscle to help. In fact, they’d probably torment you with one of their horrible gurgling laughs while you desperately gap for air. Babies simply don’t care about anyone or anything.

# 5. They erupt from human bodies like some kind of horrible infection

This one, I think, everyone can agree, is pretty rude.

# 6. They are noisy

Babies will cry all the time. Trying to prepare for that big presentation tomorrow? Incessant babbling. Trying to have a serious conversation? Nonstop wailing. Just trying to get a decent night’s sleep? The crying will never stop. It’s simply criminal behavior, but somehow they get away with it.

# 7. They murder kittens for fun

Everyone loves cute, fluffy kittens. Except babies. Babies will strangle kittens just because they feel like it.
