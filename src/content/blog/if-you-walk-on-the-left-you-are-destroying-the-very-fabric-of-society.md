Society is built upon a few very simple tenets that together allow us to live in relative peace and harmony with people we would otherwise drop kick and stuff in dumpsters. These tenants are:

1. Stay to your right.
2. Use headphones when listening to music in public.
3. Teenagers are the worst human beings that there are.

Now, most of these are unproblematic; people agree with them and act accordingly. The one that seems to be the most difficult for some people to abide is #1. Drivers get it, of course, at least in civilized countries. But pedestrians by and large simply ignore it, and it’s destroying America and the planet.

Every time you walk on the left side of the sidewalk, not only are you disrupting the flow of foot traffic, you are also murdering innocent babies and giving drugs to puppies. Not only might the person coming the other direction really have to pee, and because you’re in the way, might die of kidney failure, but you’ve also essentially stomped on a little boy’s birthday cake.

You may not believe me when I list the consequences of your sin, and I understand. When someone starts making abstract assertions, it’s easy to wave it off as “superstition,” like climate change. So let me try another tack: many of the greatest people in history were right side walkers, while all of the most villainous monsters walked on the left.

### Noted right-walkers

* Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi
* Mother Teresa
* Martin Luther King Jr.
* Joe DiMaggio
* Nelson Mandela

### Noted left-walkers

* Nero
* Stalin
* Hitler
* Saddam Hussein
* Osama bin Laden
* Nickelback

If you think that the former list is better in any way than the latter, then please walk on the right. Otherwise, evil wins.
