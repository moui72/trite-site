The angle of your legs when you sit on the subway can say a lot about your personality. Get out your protractor! Here’s what your angle say about you.

**0° to 30°**

You probably don’t have male genitalia. You like to take up as little space as possible. You’ve possibly been conditioned this way by lifelong exposure to systemic sexism.

**31° to 50°**

You feel like you deserve as much space as anyone else, but you also want to be considerate to others. You’re a fairly normal person who just wants to ride the subway without too much discomfort for you or your fellow passengers.

**51° to 90°**

You’re an asshole. You think you deserve more than other people. You’re probably trying to compensate for something. No one will ever love you.

**91° to 180°**

You are Gumby. You have a friend who is an orange horse. Your voice sounds like nightmares.

**181° or more**

You’re probably in pain. You might need to see a doctor. You sometimes have a hard time walking.
